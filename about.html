01 // VISION

ECOSYSTEM
OF SOLUTIONS.

Our vision is inclined to create an ecosystem for solutions related to the tropical climate. This integrates the product design aspect tailored for this specific climate, the practical design solutions developed, and the research aspect which deals with these solutions from a theoretical standpoint.

02 // BACKGROUND

Harsh Realities.

Tropical climates face unique challenges: high humidity, intense heat, and heavy rainfall. While traditional and passive architectural approaches historically managed these issues, modern structures often fail here. It is critical to revisit and innovate upon these foundational methods to build structures that truly breathe.
