## 📝 Key Findings

### Ventilation Strategies
- Indochina-style buildings use **cross-ventilation** through strategically placed openings
- **Stack effect** utilized through high ceilings and roof vents
- **Internal courtyards** create microclimates

### Daylighting Techniques
- **Deep overhangs** provide shade while allowing indirect light
- **Louvered windows** control light and airflow simultaneously
- **Light shelves** bounce light deeper into interiors

### Performance Metrics
- **2-4°C temperature reduction** compared to modern generic designs
- **40-60% reduction** in cooling energy consumption
- **Improved thermal comfort** without mechanical systems
