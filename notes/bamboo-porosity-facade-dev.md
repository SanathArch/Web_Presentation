# Product Development Log: Bamboo Porosity Facade

## 🎯 Product Overview
**Description:** Modular facade system using natural bamboo to achieve optimal 20% porosity for tropical climate cross-ventilation

**Target Price:** $2,800 - $3,500 (per 10m² module)

**Target Market:** 
- Residential builders in tropical regions
- Commercial developments seeking green certification
- NGO housing projects

## 🔬 Technical Specifications

### Materials
- **Primary:** Treated bamboo slats (25mm width)
- **Frame:** Powder-coated aluminum
- **Fasteners:** Stainless steel (corrosion-resistant)

### Dimensions
- **Module size:** 1.2m x 2.4m (standard)
- **Thickness:** 80mm (including frame)
- **Weight:** 18 kg/m²

### Performance Targets
- **Porosity:** 20% ±2%
- **Wind load resistance:** 120 km/h
- **UV resistance:** 5+ years
- **Water resistance:** Monsoon-grade

## 🧪 Testing Results

### Lab Testing (Completed)
- ✅ Porosity measurement: 19.8% achieved
- ✅ UV exposure test: Minimal degradation after 500hrs
- ⏳ Wind load test: Scheduled May 2026

### Field Testing (Pilot Sites)
- **Location:** Jakarta, IDN (pending installation)
- **Duration:** 3 months (July-September 2026)
- **Metrics to track:**
  - Indoor temperature reduction
  - Airflow improvement
  - Durability in monsoon conditions
