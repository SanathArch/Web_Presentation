## 📝 Key Findings

### Porosity Percentages
- **15-25% facade porosity** = optimal thermal comfort
- Below 10% = insufficient ventilation
- Above 30% = structural concerns, excessive heat gain

### Climate-Specific Data
- Study conducted in **Amazon basin** (similar to SE Asia tropics)
- **Peak summer months** tested (March-May)
- **Average temperature reduction:** 3-5°C with 20% porosity

### Material Performance
- **Bamboo screens:** Best natural material, 18-22% porosity
- **Perforated metal:** Precise control, 15-25% achievable
- **Concrete jali:** Traditional, 20-30% porosity

## 💡 Insights for Project

### For Bamboo Porosity Facade Product
