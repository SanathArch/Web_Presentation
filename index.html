02 // The Department

Tropical Climate Dept.

Fundamentals & Challenges

Tropical climates present unique architectural hurdles: intense heat, extreme humidity, and heavy monsoon rains. Modern design often ignores these factors. We analyze these challenges to create targeted, passive solutions that work with the environment.

Architectural Integration

Tropical climate architecture goes beyond aesthetics. It's about designing buildings that actively manage airflow, provide deep shading, and utilize materials that resist rot and humidity. Bringing vernacular wisdom into the 21st century.
